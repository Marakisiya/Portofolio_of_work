Branding and Packaging



This project involves the the design of a branding and packaging for a dog food product. The assigment contain four main parts; designing a logo, creating an illustrated infographic, designing a package for the product, and designing a point-of-sale for the product.

I started by designing a logo for the dog food product. I wanted a logo that presents the idea of a dog food in a simple way. Something that is recognizeable and relateable. I went with a brand name POOS and create a subtle image of dog in the logo.

For the infographic, I created a design that is easy to read and understand. I used tables, charts and pictures to present important information about the product. I used the brand color strategically to create brand awareness without overshadowing the content of the infographic. The font type and size to create a modern feelings for the infographic.

For the packaging, I decided to create a design that is attractive and eye catching given that my product is going to be displayed on the shelf in a store.

The Point of Sales design was bit of a challenge for me. This is because I have no experience working with 3D design. But eventually I tried to make it simple, modern and attractive. I used a modern color and picture and sales discount information to attract customer to it .

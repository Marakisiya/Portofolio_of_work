Information Design



This project involves the design of a poster and a website to inform people how to discover Bergen city on bike.

I designed a logo for the poster and website. I went with the concept of a logo that presents the idea of cycling in Bergen. Something that is recognizeable and relateable

I draw up a simple and modern design for poster as shown in the image on the left. The PDF file of the poster can be found here. The poster colors were carefully chosen not to overshadow the content of the poster. The font type and size complement the layout to create a modern feelings for the poster.

I design a website that is interactive and contains a Home, About, Route and Contact page. On the route page, I ensure the design included the routes to the following major areas (Landås, Fana, Fyllingsdals, Laksevåg ), a map, distances and estimated time to each routes, type of road, intersection and junction on the roads.

Website Prototyping



This project involves the design of a simple but interactive website prototype for promoting the brand identity of GrumpyGuerilla socks and to provide users with information about socks. The targeted audience include male and female between the age of 25 and 36, who like to feel and look good.

In creating the website, I consistently used the brand color in all the prominent location of the website, to create brand awareness. For example, the header and footer area of all the pages are colored green, and a black colored logo of the company placed on right hand side. This allows customer see and remember the brand when they visit the site.

I also used photos strategically on the web pages to make bold statements about the company and its products. The choice of photos should appeal to the targeted audience. I categorized the product section into male and female, with each section containing socks suitable for the type of gender. Thus, creating clarity of information and ease of finding the right product on the website.

To allow easy navigation and interactivity, I use buttons where necessary on the pages of the website. For example. On the home page there are four button at the top of the page that user can click to go directly to other pages. Also, there is another important button named “shop here” that takes user to the socks section directly.
